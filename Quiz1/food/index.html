banyuwangi local food

Tempong Rice

Has a characteristic smell of kencur from the sambal and is accompanied by a mixture of boiled vegetables, side dishes, and fried fish

Cit Egg

Cit egg is a dish made from boiled chicken eggs and then seasoned with petis so that the color looks dark brown

Rujak Soto

It is a combination of vegetable salad and beef or chicken soup. Banyuwangi people usually eat this when the price of meat goes up

Pecel Rawon

This is a combination of vegetable pecel sauce with rawon beef soup
