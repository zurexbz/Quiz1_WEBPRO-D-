about banyuwangi

Banyuwangi is one of the districts in East Java, Indonesia. The capital city is in Banyuwangi District or often called Banyuwangi City. This district is located at the easternmost tip of Java Island, in the Horseshoe area

Banyuwangi has many tourist destinations such as Ijen Crater, Alas Purwo National Park, Red Island, etc. In addition, Banyuwangi has a variety of cultures ranging from food, dance, etc

local food

Tempong Rice

Has a characteristic smell of kencur from the sambal and is accompanied by a mixture of boiled vegetables, side dishes, and fried fish

Cit Egg

Cit egg is a dish made from boiled chicken eggs and then seasoned with petis so that the color looks dark brown

Rujak Soto

It is a combination of vegetable salad and beef or chicken soup. Banyuwangi people usually eat this when the price of meat goes up

Pecel Rawon

This is a combination of vegetable pecel sauce with rawon beef soup
